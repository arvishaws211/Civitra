Understanding the Process

What is the Election Commission of India (ECI)?

The ECI is an autonomous constitutional authority responsible for administering election processes in India at national and state levels.

Who is eligible to vote?

Any Indian citizen who is 18 years or older on the qualifying date (usually Jan 1st of the year) and is enrolled in the electoral roll can vote.

What documents do I need?

You need your Voter ID card (EPIC). If you don't have it, you can use other approved photo IDs like an Aadhaar Card, PAN card, or Passport, provided your name is on the electoral roll.
